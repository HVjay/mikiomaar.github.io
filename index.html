A FUSION OF STRATEGY, GEOMAPPING, AND AR

WAR WAGES IN MIKIOMA

The Mikioma Kingdom, the rulers of the Land of Aonar, led by Pontifex Lambda are in constant conflict with the Pinto Rebellion, led by their commander Harris of the Hammer. Once a hero of Kingdom, Harris was distraught by the atrocities the Mikioma Kingdom committed against their foes under Lambda’s command. Knowing that his efforts would be in vain were he to take it upon himself to overthrow the leader, Harris and his loyal comrades banded to bring justice to the kingdom. Both factions find themselves fighting monsters originating from the Quinto region. Living in the dark shadows of the realm are the Jonkofs, a mysterious, invisible tribe consisting of evasive rogues. Additionally, all the groups seem destined to fight forever, unless a new hero can emerge and lead their group to eternal glory.

WHY MIKIOMA?

  • Get active while still playing the MMORPG games you love
  • Enjoy classic MMORPG experience on a mobile/AR platform
  • Unlike other AR games, sophisticated game mechanics so you don't lose interest after a few hours
  • Meet new people and explore your town while engaging in nonrepetitive content
  • Be immersed in a dynamic game world that is unique to you

FEATURES

  • Align yourself as elves, dwarves, orcs, humans, or dragonkin
  • Challenge your friends or fight enemies to claim real world territories for your race
  • Master strategic advantages, such as weather patterns and times, to conquer your opponents
  • An AR game that emphasizes well-developed game systems, not just geomapping
  • Earn skillpoints to differentiate and show off your character
